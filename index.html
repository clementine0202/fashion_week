1. Origines

Since its creation in 1973, Paris Fashion Week has been a must-attend fashion event for fashion lovers from all over the world. The Paris edition was the first in the line of fashion weeks, and is now part of the so-called "Big 4", joining Milan, London and New York.

2. Creator

The creator of this unmissable event is a female designer ! Fashion Week was conceived and invented in New York in 1943 by a famous fashion publicist, Eleanor Lambert.
